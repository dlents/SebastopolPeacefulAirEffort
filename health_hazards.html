The Sebastopol Peaceful Air Effort was initiated by Shepherd Bliss and Jonathan Greenberg to respond to the toxic, disruptive impact that the regulated use of leaf blowers is having on our community. There is overwhelming evidence, some of it presented on this website, that leaf blowers are the most unnecessary, easy-to-avoid polluters in our lives. It is time to stop this insane imposition on our health.

A century ago, Supreme Court Justice Oliver Wendell Holmes ruled that “The right to swing my fist ends where the other man's nose begins. The other citizen’s nose, as well as our eardrums, and our lungs, are what we are seeking to protect when we urge our local elected officials to enact the an effective ban on leaf blowers in Sebastopol.
